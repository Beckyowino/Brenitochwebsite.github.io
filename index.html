Welcome to Brenitoch Schools

Brenitoch Primary School is a distinguished independent private school offering the Competency Based Curriculum (CBC) for Pre-Primary, Primary, and Junior Secondary levels. We provide a nurturing environment that encourages academic excellence, creativity, and moral growth, preparing our students for success in an ever-changing world. Our modern facilities, experienced educators, and commitment to holistic development set us apart.

Why Choose Brenitoch School?

Brenitoch Primary School provides a character-focused, values-driven education through a school-wide Global Citizenship programme.

As a Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) school, Brenitoch promotes experiential learning opportunities for all students through local, regional, and international excursions.

Student leadership opportunities are encouraged in the Brenitoch School community to ensure our graduates are well-prepared to thrive with humility and an appreciation of social responsibility.
