A home Away From home

At Brenitoch School, we understand that boarding accommodation is more than just a place to sleep—it's a home away from home where students grow, learn, and thrive. Our modern boarding facilities are designed to provide a safe, comfortable, and nurturing environment that supports both academic success and personal development.

Our boarding program offers students the unique opportunity to develop independence, build lasting friendships, and benefit from extended learning time with access to study halls, libraries, and academic support in the evenings. With experienced house parents and matrons providing round-the-clock care, parents can rest assured that their children are in safe, caring hands.

Boarding Facilities Features

  • Spacious, well-ventilated dormitories with comfortable beds and storage
  • Separate accommodation blocks for boys and girls with dedicated supervision
  • Modern bathrooms and sanitation facilities maintained to high standards
  • Nutritious meals prepared by trained kitchen staff (breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks)
  • Common rooms for recreation and relaxation with TV and games
  • Study halls with adequate lighting and quiet environment for evening prep
  • On-site medical facility with qualified nurse for health emergencies
  • Laundry services and personal care facilities
